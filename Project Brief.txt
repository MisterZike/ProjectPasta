------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                                   PROJECT PASTA
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Objective:
Design and create a web application that provides recipes and ingredients for a desired dish, with prices and nutrition
information.

Requirements:
1) Web interface
2) Ability for the user to type their desired dish
3) Ability to provide a list of required ingredients with quantities/measurements
4) Ability to calculate and display costs to purchase ingredients
5) Ability to display an estimate of total nutritional information based on the ingredients
